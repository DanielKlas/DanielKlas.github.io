Treasure Hunt Mobile Web App


As part of my internship at Amazon, I have designed and implemented a Web App aimed at providing active leisure to the office staff, as well as increasing engagement. Treasure Hunt is a scavenger-hunt-like game that displays clues to finding treasure after scanning the right QR codes found around the Amazon center. It can also be used to train new staff in the vast space of the Amazon warehouses.
